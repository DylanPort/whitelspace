Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ghost Whistle ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Ghost Whistle is a decentralized privacy network built on the Solana blockchain. We prioritize user privacy and minimize data collection wherever possible.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Wallet Address: When you connect your Solana wallet, we process your public wallet address to enable staking, rewards, and node management.
  • Node Information: If you run privacy nodes, we collect node performance data (uptime, bandwidth, connections) for reward distribution.
  • Chat Messages: Messages sent in Ghost Terminal (exclusive staker chat) are temporarily stored for delivery. We do not permanently archive chat history.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, time spent (for improving user experience).
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version.
  • Network Information: IP addresses and network status (only when running nodes to facilitate P2P connections).

2.3 Blockchain Data

  • On-Chain Transactions: All staking, unstaking, and reward transactions are publicly recorded on the Solana blockchain.
  • Smart Contract Interactions: Your interactions with Ghost Whistle smart contracts are transparent and immutable on-chain.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: Processing staking transactions, distributing rewards, managing nodes.
  • Communication: Sending notifications about rewards, node status, and network updates.
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance app features and user experience.
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and security threats.
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service.

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 We DO Share

  • Blockchain Networks: Transaction data is shared with the Solana blockchain (public ledger).
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation.

4.2 We DO NOT Share

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not share your information for advertising purposes.
  • We do not disclose your private keys or wallet credentials (we never have access to them).

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmissions use TLS/SSL encryption.
  • Access Controls: Strict access limitations to user data within our team.
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use reputable cloud providers with robust security practices.
  • No Private Key Storage: We never store or have access to your wallet private keys.

Important: While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your wallet credentials and seed phrases secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of non-essential communications.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@ghostwhistle.com

7. Data Retention

  • Wallet Addresses: Retained while you use the service and for a reasonable period after.
  • Node Performance Data: Retained for up to 12 months for reward distribution and auditing.
  • Chat Messages: Retained temporarily (up to 30 days) for delivery, then deleted.
  • Blockchain Data: Permanently recorded on Solana blockchain (immutable).

8. Children's Privacy

Ghost Whistle is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at privacy@ghostwhistle.com.

9. International Users

Ghost Whistle operates globally. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries where we operate, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

10. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with third-party services:

  • Wallet Providers: Phantom, Solflare, etc. (governed by their own privacy policies).
  • Blockchain RPC Providers: For Solana network connectivity.
  • Analytics Services: For app performance monitoring.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies.

Continued use of Ghost Whistle after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
